Summary

Proyect Invasion is a Free mobile game made by spanish students for academic principles. It is a Top-Down shoot-em up mobile game where a space soldier has to survive and kill hordes of aliens by waves. earning upgrades such as multi-bullet, more damage, explosive ammo, speed boost, life regeneration and more.



My contribution

Enemies

Three types of enemies: Melee, Tank and Ranged
AI movement with Unity Navigation System
Wave management
Mini-bosses that have more health and damage, and give more experience
Melee attack, Ranged shoots and Tank stun
Animation implementation

Game progression

Each round enemies are stronger and map changes
On map some slow and spike traps appear
Enemies have more damage and health, adapting to player power-ups
Waves also adapts, so more enemies appear varying the type and position

Save System

Player can save between rounds, as the game is thought to be casual to kill time
Saves player power-ups, stats and enemies state
